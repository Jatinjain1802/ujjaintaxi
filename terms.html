Our Standard Carriage Rules

Please review these structural terms before booking your journey. They ensure safe, smooth, and predictable travel standards.

01

Maximum Mileage Average: Outstation bookings hold a standard minimum average calculation of 300 Kilometers per calendar day.

02

Toll & State Taxes: Local Toll taxes, state border permits, passenger taxes, and parking fees will be paid directly by the traveler party.

03

Advance Retention: Booking deposit and advances are strictly non-refundable in the event of customer cancellation.

04 -->

Seating Safety: The carriage vehicle will not accommodate any passengers beyond its formal registered seating capacity limits.

05

Baggage Responsibility: The traveling party is fully responsible for all personal bags, electronic goods, and luggage carried inside the cab.

06

Contradiction Expenses: All highway route changes and detours requested by the party will subject them to extra state taxes or toll fares.

07

Calendar Day Limits: Outstation cabs operate on a calendar date system. Bookings extending past 12:00 midnight automatically trigger the next day's rental rate.

08

Minimum Mileage Charge: If the vehicle runs less than the daily standard kilometer averages, the billing is calculated according to the minimum daily averages.

09

Penalty & Challan: The customer party will be responsible for any administrative police penalties arising from wrong route guidance or speed instructions forced upon the driver.

10

Night Allowances: Driver night allowances are applicable based on hours and outstation conditions. The standard charge ranges between ₹300 to ₹500.
