About Bada Ganesh Temple

Located near the Mahakaleshwar Temple, the Bade Ganeshji Ka Mandir houses a massive and highly ornate idol of Lord Ganesha.

Highlights

An interesting feature of this temple is that the idol of Lord Ganesha is not made of standard stone or clay, but is constructed using jaggery, bricks, and limestone.

Astrology Hub

The temple is not just a place of worship but also serves as an important center for learning Sanskrit and astrology in Ujjain.
